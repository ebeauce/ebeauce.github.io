Welcome !

I am a graduate student of the Geophysics Program in the Earth, Atmospheric and Planetary Sciences (EAPS) department at MIT.

My work, supervised by Michel Campillo, focuses on the development of methods for automatic earthquake detection. I am particularly interested in studying how the large amount of seismic data can be processed with modern techniques emerging from machine learning.
