Earthquake Catalogs

Here is a compilation of the earthquake catalogs that my collaborators and myself have built with different variants of the BPMF workflow.

Ridgecrest, California, 2009-2019 (Beaucé et al. 2023)

-- Latest version (v2.0.1)

The catalog v2.0.1 was created with BPMF v2.0.1 and can be found at: https://zenodo.org/records/8393318.

-- Original version (v1.0.0)

The original version of the catalog, v1.0.0, was created with BPMF v2.0.0 and can be found at: https://zenodo.org/records/8127880.

North Anatolian Fault, 2012-2013 (Beaucé et al. 2022)

This catalog was built with a proto version of BPMF. It can be found at https://zenodo.org/records/6362973.

Western Alps, 2012-2013 (Beaucé et al. 2019)

This catalog was built with a proto version of BPMF. Catalogs are given as h5 files.

  • Origin times, locations and P/S-wave travel times given per template: catalog_Beauce_et_al_2019_per_template.h5
  • Origin times, locations and P/S-wave travel times of the 18,754 earthquakes presented in Beaucé et al. 2019: catalog_Beauce_et_al_2019.h5
