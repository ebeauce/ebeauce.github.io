Material by Eric Beaucé

Earthquake catalog from Beaucé et al. 2019

Catalogs are given as h5 files.

  • Origin times, locations and P/S-wave travel times given per template: catalog_Beauce_et_al_2019_per_template.h5
  • Origin times, locations and P/S-wave travel times of the 18,754 earthquakes presented in Beaucé et al. 2019: catalog_Beauce_et_al_2019.h5

Reference:

  • Eric Beaucé, William B. Frank, Anne Paul, Michel Campillo and Robert D. van der Hilst. Systematic Detection of Clustered Seismicity Beneath the Southwestern Alps. Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1029/2019JB018110.
